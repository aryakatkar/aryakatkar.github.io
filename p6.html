SKINCARE BRAND


I chose to create a skincare brand based on what I feel like is needed in the market. Most skincare brands now are all about fancy products, trends and aesthetics, there is no full transparency about the ingredients and it is not catered with the intention of helping skin to be bette. I wanted to create a brand that is clean, science backed research, transparent, very minimal skincare that is efficient and suitable for all skin types, especially sensitive skin.
