VINYL CCOVER REDESIGN


A modern vinyl record cover design for Black Sabbath’s album ‘Paranoid’ that was released in 1970. The album talks about the themes of existentialism, paranoia, war and politics and I wanted to incorporate that in the design. I have used a red and black color scheme to depict paranoia, and yellow for the typography to make it cohesive. The design has references to two songs that I had specifically in mind from the album, ‘Paranoia’ and ‘War Pigs’.
