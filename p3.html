STUDIO GHIBLI COOKBOOK


This cookbook is based on the animated films by Studio Ghibli. It is a Japanese animation studio created by Hayao Miyazaki - renown for its distinct and easygoing animation, as well as romanticization of the simple things in life. It contains 6 curated recipes, each from different movies, categorised into- breakfast, lunch/dinner, dessert. I wanted the design to speak bold, minimalist, timeless and comforting. Taking inspirations from bold Japanese typography, publications and designers, I decided on that sense. For the colours, I wanted each recipe spread to embody the colour scheme of the film it corresponds to.
